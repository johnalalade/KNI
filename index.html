Meet the set-man

For every move of God on earth, a man is set in place to implement...

Pastor Gabriel Fagbule

Pastor Gabriel Fagbule is a young anointed minister of the gospel with the mandate to raise a generation of Kings and Priests. He hails from Ile Ife, Osun State, Nigeria. He came from a priesthood family. In 2014 through the ministry of Apostle Joshua Selman (Eternity Network International), he started having visionary encounters which brought him into a deeper experience of the personality of Jesus Christ and the ministry of the Holy Spirit. Since then he has been on fire for God using every of his God's given gifts to advance the purposes of God. He has served in different leadership capacities both in Christian fold and secular settings. As a university student, he served as secretary of a fellowship and later served as the pastor of the same fellowship. He has served as youth president and also pastored a youth Church before God entrusted him with the mandate of Kingdom Network International. He was the host of "Command Your Day" online morning prayers held between early 2021 till early 2022 which had great testimonies. Pastor Gabriel holds a Bachelor of Arts Degree in Philosophy from the prestigious institution Obafemi Awolowo University, Ile Ife. He has been to World Bible School College USA and is also a graduate of Koinonia School of Ministry Abuja under the leadership of Apostle Joshua Selman. He is skilled in graphics, media, and public speaking. A lover of a good and quality gospel sound. He is also a musician. He's influenced by the ministries of men like Apostle Joshua Selman, Dr. Myles Munroe (BM), Bishop David Oyedepo, Pastor Sam Adeyemi, and many more. His spiritual father is Apostle Joshua Selman. Pastor Gabriel is the Founder and President of Kingdom Network International.
